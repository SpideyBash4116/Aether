# :warning: Alpha Notice
**Aether** is currently in early alpha stage (v0.1.2-alpha.) Expect bugs, incomplete features, and occasional breaking changes — the language and runtime are under active, frequent development.

## :bookmark_tabs:  Recommendations
-	Save your work often with save <file> and keep external backups of important data.
-	Pull the latest changes from main before starting a session when possible.
-	Do not rely on Aether for production or critical workloads.

## :writing_hand: Reporting issues
1.	Open an issue at https://github.com/SpideyBash4116/Aether/issues.
2.	Provide a short description, exact reproduction steps, and any error output.
3.	Include the Aether version shown at startup, your OS/platform, and relevant logs.

## :rocket: Release expectations
-	Updates and fixes are released frequently and may introduce breaking changes.
-	Alpha releases are intended for experimentation and feedback.

### Thank you for testing Aether — your reports and feedback help stabilize the project.
